Welcome to **Artless Deep Learning**! This category is dedicated to breaking down the complexities of deep learning into simple, understandable concepts. Much of deep learning remains poorly understood, and architectural choices are often chosen either arbitrarily, based on dogma, by trial and error, or all of the above. Here, we strip away the mystique and get to the heart of why and how deep learning works. Whether it's understanding the rationale behind selecting certain hyperparameters or choosing sane model architectures, our goal is to make deep learning accessible and clear.

Stay tuned for posts that will help you navigate the world of deep learning with confidence and clarity, without the unnecessary frills.
